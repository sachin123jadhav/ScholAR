Integrate with your LMS

Labster integrates with all major LMS (Learning Management Systems) so that educators can use their gradebooks to track students’ performance data and students can keep a record of their work. Labster is compatible with Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, Google Classroom, Schoology, Sakai, and Brightspace / D2L. It’s also possible to use Labster without an LMS.

Taking smart technology to the class & beyond

ScholAR is an advanced virtual lab, designed to provide a digital platform for conducting a variety of interactive science experiments. Many of these experiments are based on real life scenarios.

Hands-on learning experience

We are on mission to digitize experiential learning. SchoolAR commits to providing a superlative hands-on experience in a virtual environment. Our emphasis is on “Learning by Doing”

Best in class STEM tool

SchoolAR leverages the power of 3D visualizations to simplify complex concepts in science. Our goal is to intensify engagement levels, and make the topics interesting, even though some of them may be very theoretical or abstract.

Application oriented

SchoolAR encourages students to apply the science conceps to real world scenarios. This is the crucial stepping stone to creating innovators in our society.
